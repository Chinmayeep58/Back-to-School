Games
The games section is the most loved one, both teachers and students alike. So let's dive into the world of the most intriguing games.

(hover over the cards to explore the game.)

Collective Nouns

Instructions:

Form a group and perform an action representing a collective noun (e.g., 'a flock of birds'). The class has 30 seconds to guess the collective noun based on the group's actions. Repeat with different groups and actions.

Taboo!!

Instructions

One player receives a word and must make the team guess it without saying the restricted list words. They have 60 seconds. Repeat with different players and words.

Dumb Charades

Instructions

Divide into teams. One player acts out a vacation place they've visited or liked without speaking. Team guesses using gestures and movements. Set a time limit. Rotate players and repeat.

2 Truth 1 Lie

Instructions

The students have to tell 3 facts about their life that happened to them in the past. Two of them should be true, and one should be a lie. The other students have to find out which one is the lie. You'll be surprised what kind of crazy things can actually happen!

Word Association

Instructions

A student says a word, and the next student quickly responds with the first word that comes to mind, continuing around the room.

Rap

Instructions

Teams create a rap or song using words given by the teacher. They have 2 minutes to perform. Rotate words and repeat with different teams.
